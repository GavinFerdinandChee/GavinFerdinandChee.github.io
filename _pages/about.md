Hello there! I'm Gavin and thank you for visiting my online portfolio.
I'm a Mechatronics and Robotics engineer based in Singapore, and I'm currently studying for a BEng (Hons) in Mechanical Engineering with a Mechatronics specialization.

I currently hold a Diploma in Mechatronics and Robotics and have worked on several large-scale projects, including the development of a solar-powered cruiser class vehicle for the Bridgestone World Solar Challenge.

In my spare time, I enjoy gaming, homelabbing, CAD modelling, coding, tinkering with my 3D Printers, and petting the stray cats in my neighbourhood.
